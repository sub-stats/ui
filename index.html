What Our App Is

Better Sub Stats is a better stat tracking method for tracking user discussion over time

As you can see by the bar graph to the rigt. We provide an easy to use visual interface to track the data that's important to YOU

Features

Better Sub Stats gives you the ability to sort by:

  • Posts per Window (of time)
  • Comments per Post
  • Find most active times per Window
  • Time-series plots of post counts and comment counts
  • Time-series plot of comments per post
  • 'Trending' Score of (# of followers) / (age of sub)
